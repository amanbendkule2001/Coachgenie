
# 📚 SmartEdu – Tutor Management Platform

SmartEdu is a platform designed to help tutors manage their teaching workflow in a structured and efficient way.
It brings together student management, academic tracking, and administrative tools into one centralized system.

The goal of SmartEdu is to simplify how tutors organize their classes, monitor student progress, and handle daily academic operations.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

SmartEdu aims to provide tutors with a digital workspace where they can manage both academic and operational tasks without relying on multiple tools.

With SmartEdu, tutors can:

* 👨‍🏫 Manage students and academic records
* 🏫 Organize courses, subjects, and batches
* 📝 Create tests and track marks & rankings
* 💰 Manage fees, payments, and receipts
* 📅 Plan timetables, activities, and holidays
* 📊 Track student performance and analytics
* 📞 Manage enquiries and lead follow-ups

The platform is being built as a **scalable and modular system**, allowing it to grow with additional features and integrations in the future.
